About Me

I'm a Tokyo-based AI Researcher who specializes in AI Healtcare application using IoT devices. My research focused on activity detection IoT application.

I obtained my Ph.D. degree from the Keio University, Japan in 2022 under the supervision of Prof. Tomoaki ohtsuki. My research mainly based on assistive health care technology with infrared imaging.
